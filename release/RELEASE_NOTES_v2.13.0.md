# StudyBar 2.13 — Fullscreen focus mode

Expand the floating timer into a distraction-free fullscreen HUD — big ring, live countdown, pause/stop. Menu bar popover stays unchanged.

## What's new in 2.13

### Floating timer fullscreen
- Tap **↗** on the on-screen HUD to fill the screen during an active session
- Large progress ring, time remaining, subject label, pause/stop controls
- Tap **↙** or close to return to the compact floating panel
- Same draggable HUD as before when not expanded

## Upgrade from 2.12.x

Download the DMG from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/IMisbahk/studybar/releases/tag/v2.13.0), replace the app in Applications, or use in-app update when idle.

Your subjects, history, goals, themes, and achievements are unchanged.
