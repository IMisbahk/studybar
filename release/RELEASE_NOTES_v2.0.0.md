# StudyBar 2.0 — Production Ready

StudyBar 2.0 is the complete study timer experience: start sessions from the menu bar, track your progress, unlock achievements, and understand your habits — all on your Mac, with no account and no cloud.

## What's new in 2.0

### Smarter starting
- **Suggested session length** — one tap to use the duration that matches your study history
- **Pinned subjects** — star up to 3 subjects; they appear first in the picker and on the start screen
- **First-launch onboarding** — add a subject, enable notifications, set a daily goal, open the dashboard

### Goals & motivation
- **Daily and weekly study goals** — progress rings on the timer popover and dashboard overview
- Adjustable in Settings → Study Goals (default: 45 min/day, 300 min/week)

### Smarter reminders
- **Peak focus time** — daily notification at your most productive hour
- **Inactivity nudges** — gentle reminder when you haven't studied in a while
- **Sunday week recap** — weekly summary notification every Sunday at 6 PM
- **Pause nudge** — notification if a session stays paused for 10+ minutes

### Data you own
- **Backup & restore** — export a zip of your full database to Downloads; restore from Settings
- **Session log** — delete test sessions or bulk-remove sessions under 5 minutes (Timeline → Session log)

### Insights & analytics (from 1.8)
- **Smart Insights** tab — local pattern detection, burnout warnings, weekly/monthly summaries
- Full analytics dashboard with heatmap, charts, and exports

### Gamification (from 1.7)
- XP, levels, achievements, and galaxy view

### Timeline (from 1.6)
- Visual day-by-day replay with pause segments and filters

### Automatic updates
- Checks GitHub releases **every 6 hours**
- Downloads and installs when **no session is running** (toggle in Settings → Updates)
- Manual "Restart to Update" still available

## Privacy
- No accounts, no telemetry, no cloud
- All data stays in `~/Library/Application Support/StudyBar/`

## Requirements
- macOS 14.0 or later

## Install
Download `StudyBar-2.0.0.dmg` from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/IMisbahk/studybar/releases), drag to Applications, and launch.
