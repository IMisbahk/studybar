# StudyBar 2.13.5 — Ambient sounds

Study with offline procedural ambient audio — no bundled files, no network. Pick a sound on the timer tab or in Settings; auto-plays during sessions when enabled.

## What's new in 2.13.5

### Ambient sounds
Seven procedural presets generated locally via `AVAudioEngine`:

- White noise, pink noise
- Rain, storm, ocean
- Café, fan

Volume slider and auto-play toggle in Settings. Picker on the idle timer tab (2-column grid).

### Popover polish
- Fixed **300×463** pane across Timer, Timeline, and Settings tabs
- Subject & topic pickers side-by-side on idle setup
- Subject shown below timer ring during active sessions
- Start Session moved above ambient picker so the primary action stays visible

## Upgrade from 2.13.0

Download the DMG from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/IMisbahk/studybar/releases/latest), replace the app in Applications, or use in-app update when idle.

Your subjects, history, goals, themes, and achievements are unchanged.
